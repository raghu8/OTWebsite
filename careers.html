The domain of Information Technology Services is a highly complex and competitive industry. In the 21st century the industry expectations are higher than ever. We hold that a minimum of a bachelor's degree in a STEM field is required to have the desired skills and abilities. Thus, as a company we hold ourselves to the industry standard and require a minimum of a bachelor's degree for all non-training technical positions. We accept degrees in Computer Applications, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Information Systems, Software Engineering, Electronics, Mathematics, Engineering, 0r the U.S. equivalent in a closely related field.
